View Pinetop Campground Rules (PDF)

Details

  • Date: Friday August 29th (1 pm) - Saturday August 30th (11am)
  • Location: Pinetop West Campground
    150 Pinetop Rd.
    Idaho City, ID 83716
  • Food: The ward will provide breakfast. Please plan accordingly for your own meals.
  • Please bring a Friday dinner that is easy to make over a campfire.

Activities

  • Friday, August 29thYou may arrive at 1pm or later
  • Basketball/Picketball- 1:15pm-4:15pm
  • Grassy Field-All Day
    • Kickball
    • Ultimate Frisbee
    • Capture the Flag
    • Glowstick
  • Pavilion-All Day
    • Board Games
    • Card Games
    • Puzzles
  • Campfire- 8:00pm-9:30pm
    • Testimony Meeting
    • S'mores-Please bring your own marshmallow sticks
  • Saturday, August 30th
    • Breakfast- 8:00am-9:30am
    • Pavilion- 8:00am-11:00am
    • Packed out no later than 11:00am
  • No swimming will be allowed due to construction

What to Bring

  • Camping gear (tent, sleeping bag, bedding.)
  • If you are in the bunkhouse-please bring sheets, pillows, and foam roll
  • Personal items (toiletries, medications, etc.)
  • Outdoor clothing and sturdy shoes
  • Water bottles and hydration packs
  • Flashlights or headlamps

    • For more information on rules from Pinetop, please refer to the official guidelines.
