A Crazy Start

I was planning on starting the day on Monday, but I was woken up from my wife. I had to take her to the hospital for an emergency surgery.
It was a long day. I arrived there at 3am. I didn't leave to go home until 6pm. I was exhausted. Now, I am playing catch-up.
However, life happens. I am grateful that I was able to be there for my wife. She is doing well now.
